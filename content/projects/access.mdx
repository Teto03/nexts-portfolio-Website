A mobile application designed to empower users on their journey toward financial independence and early retirement (FIRE). The app offers intuitive financial tracking, personalized insights, and advanced simulation tools to help you manage your portfolio and plan for a secure future.

- Intuitive financial tracking
- Personalized insights
- Advanced simulation tools
- Portfolio management
- Retirement planning

## Overview

Ignition Finance helps users track their financial progress toward FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) goals through a comprehensive yet user-friendly mobile interface. The application provides tools for monitoring investments, analyzing spending patterns, and projecting future financial scenarios.

## Features

- Portfolio Tracking: Monitor your investments across multiple asset classes
- Expense Management: Track and categorize spending to optimize savings
- FIRE Calculator: Simulate retirement scenarios based on current savings and investment rates
- Personalized Insights: Receive tailored recommendations to accelerate your path to financial independence
- Goal Setting: Define and track progress toward custom financial milestones

For more detailed information on the app's architecture, components, and functionalities, please refer to the documentation file located in the documentation folder. This app was developed as a project for the Programmation of Mobile Devices exam at Universita' di Milano Bicocca. The app is the result of the cooperation of the Ignition Finance team.
